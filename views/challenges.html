Challenges

Beginner's Challenge

Ivory Hacks has a title challenge aimed specifically at Freshman and Sophomore programmers who have attended less than three Hackathons. We will provide introductory classes on the latest Hackathon Tools and Code Stacks. We have publicly available starting repos and we will provide project advisors to serve as mentors and clients.

Three Tophat Challenge

Our Three Hat Challenge: Pull a frontend, backend and topic from top hats; Repeat 3 times; Select one of the combinations.

GOOD LUCK!

Ten Buzzword Challenge

No good software is complete without creative packaging. From Scalable Collaboration tools that Leverage the Power of the Cloud to Cross-Platform Mobile Solutions that use Big Data to increase Synergy, what we all actually want out of software is to sound cool describing it.

All entries must be compatible with at least 10 of our preapproved list of overused, misunderstood, inconceivable, shinny, technology buzzwords.
